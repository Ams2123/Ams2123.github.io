About Me

My work involves a crazy combination of robotics, web development, and AI automations! My journey began with the movie "Enthiran," where I was captivated by robots. That's when I set my goal: to become a robotic scientist and create humanoid robots and other types.

I took my first step in sixth standard, learning IoT and doing basic projects. In eighth, I learned to simulate materials and parts in 2D and 3D using software like AutoCAD and Solidworks. After a four-year break during high school, my goal reignited in college, where I delved into AI and web development, leading me to where I am now.

My hobbies include reading novels, making crafts, and watching thriller movies. I truly love solving complex and interesting problems, whether in math, real life, or through programming.
